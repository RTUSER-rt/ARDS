CMUH ARDS
「Lung Protective Strategy」Protocol

ARDS 2012 Berlin Definition²
Onset：1 週內發病，新發或惡化的呼吸症狀（within 7 days）
影像：雙側肺浸潤（X-ray / CT），無法以積液、肺葉塌陷或結節完全解釋
Cannot be fully explained by cardiac failure or fluid overload
Hypoxemia：PaO₂/FIO₂ ≤ 300 mmHg（PEEP ≥ 5 cmH₂O）
Mild：200 < PaO₂/FIO₂ ≤ 300 mmHg
Moderate：100 < PaO₂/FIO₂ ≤ 200 mmHg
Severe：PaO₂/FIO₂ ≤ 100 mmHg
↓PaO₂/FIO₂ ≤ 300 mmHg
Lung Protective Strategy²
Tidal volume 6 mL/kg PBW*（範圍 4–8 mL/kg）within 4 hours
Pplat ≤ 30 cmH₂O
Adequate PEEP（依下方對應表）
Goal（clinical judgment）
Oxygenation：PaO₂ 55–80 mmHg or SpO₂ 88%–95%
Ventilation：pH ≥ 7.25，Respiratory rate ≤ 35 breaths/min
Sedation if needed for ventilator synchronization
F/u arterial gas：q6h & 0.5–1 hr after adjustment
*PBW Predicted Body Weight
Male = 50 + 0.91 × (Height − 152.4) Female = 45.5 + 0.91 × (Height − 152.4)
FIO₂ / PEEP 對應表
↓PaO₂/FIO₂ < 150 mmHg
Neuromuscular Blocker⁴
Timing：Early — first 48h of mod-severe ARDS（PaO₂/FIO₂ < 150 mmHg under PEEP 5 cmH₂O）
Continuous IV infusion for ventilator synchronization（RASS target：−4 ~ −5）
Prone Position⁵
Timing：PaO₂/FIO₂ < 150 mmHg，PEEP ≥ 5 cmH₂O，FIO₂ ≥ 0.6
Duration：At least 16 hours per day
Stop criteria：
改善：Supine P/F > 150，FIO₂ ≤ 0.6，PEEP ≤ 10 cmH₂O，持續 4 hr
Worsening oxygenation
Desaturation（SpO₂ 驟降）
Pressure injury / Catheter or ETT dislodgement
Cardiac arrest / Hemodynamic instability
中榮護理部｜翻回仰臥時機
1. FiO₂ ≤ 45%，PEEP < 8 cmH₂O，SpO₂ > 90%
2. 俯臥時立即發生血壓下降
3. 血氧飽和濃度惡化
4. 嚴重併發症需翻回急救，如心律不整、氣胸⋯等

ECMO⁶
Timing：Early if no contraindications
Hypoxemia：FIO₂ > 0.8 + optimal care as above
▶ P/F ratio < 80 for 6 hr
▶ P/F ratio < 50 for 3 hr
Hypercapnia：despite RR ≤ 35 and Pplat ≤ 30 cmH₂O
▶ PaCO₂ > 60 mmHg & pH < 7.25 for 6H
During ECMO：Ultra-protective ventilation
Tidal volume：4 ml/kg PBW
▶ PEEP：10 cmH₂O
▶ Driving pressure：10 cmH₂O
▶ RR：10 bpm
▶ FIO₂：0.4
